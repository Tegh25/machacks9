BACKGROUND

As Urbanization increases more and more animals around the world are going extinct due to habitat lost, pollution, deforestation, climate change etc. The WWF found that humans are responsible for 99% of the endangered species and that the current extinction rate is 1000 to 10000 times higher than the natural rate. Animatrix is a web application which uses AI to classify animals based on pictures uploaded by the user. This application was developed in an attempt to increase awareness for endangered species of animals around the world. The goal of this application is with easy access to information regarding a specific animal kids are more eager to learn about the different species that exist out there. This application will provide the name of the animal, its species, weather its on the endangered list or not and a little description of the animal when prompted with and image.
